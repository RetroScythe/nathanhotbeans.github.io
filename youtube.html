The RetroScythe YouTube channel is where most of the RetroScythe community resides, whether you are just a viewer or are a long time follower, this is where the top quality content can be found.

[Content]
The channel is mainly focused on my old and ongoing projects. As of now, there are videos about my treehouses, homemade car and an old banger car series. Other types of content may show up from time to time such as gaming videos.

[History]
Starting in 2015, "RXCreeper13" (The original channel name) was occasionally gifted with some very crudely made videos, they were recorded and uploaded straight to youtube. Most of these videos were ROBLOX. These videos featured moments that were simply not believable, so much so that people just disliked the videos and called it all fake (Welcome to the internet!). Those videos are now private to keep the channel cleaner and have since been replaced by videos with a much higher standard for quality and entertainment value.
The channel started to gain popularity around early 2020 from Minecraft videos. the channel was mainly gaming but featured the occasional video that was non-gaming related.
In 2023, the channel was relaunched for a new type of content. The main reason was that me and my friends had lost interest in gaming and started to gain interest in our projects. From today, the channel is focused mainly on these projects.
